Cancellation and Refund Policy

Effective Date: November 17, 2024

Last Updated: November 17, 2024

30-Day Money-Back Guarantee: We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on most of our products and services. If you're not satisfied within 30 days of purchase, you can request a full refund.

1. Cancellation Policy

1.1 Subscription Services

  • You can cancel your subscription at any time through your account dashboard
  • Cancellation takes effect at the end of your current billing period
  • You will continue to have access until the end of the paid period
  • No partial refunds for unused time in the current billing period

1.2 One-Time Purchases

  • Digital products can be cancelled within 30 days of purchase
  • Physical products can be cancelled before shipping
  • Custom or personalized items cannot be cancelled once production begins

1.3 Project-Based Services

  • Cancellation allowed within 48 hours of contract signing
  • After 48 hours, cancellation fees may apply based on work completed
  • Milestone-based projects can be cancelled after any completed milestone

2. Refund Eligibility

Product/Service Type Refund Period Refund Amount Conditions
Monthly Subscriptions Within 30 days of first payment 100% First-time subscribers only
Annual Subscriptions Within 30 days 100% Minus any usage-based fees
Annual Subscriptions After 30 days Pro-rated Based on unused months

3. How to Request a Cancellation or Refund

Log into Your Account

Access your Bheem Platform dashboard using your credentials

Navigate to Billing Section

Go to Account Settings > Billing > Manage Subscriptions

Select Service to Cancel

Choose the subscription or service you wish to cancel

Submit Cancellation Request

Click "Cancel" and provide reason for cancellation (optional)

Receive Confirmation

You'll receive an email confirmation within 24 hours

Alternative Methods:

  • Email: Send request to refunds@bheem.co.uk
  • Phone: Call +44 20 7123 4567 during business hours
  • Support Ticket: Submit through your dashboard

4. Processing Time

  • Cancellation requests: Processed within 24 hours
  • Refund requests: Reviewed within 2-3 business days
  • Refund processing: 5-10 business days after approval
  • Bank processing: Additional 3-5 days depending on your bank

5. Non-Refundable Items

The following are not eligible for refunds:

  • Services already rendered or consumed
  • Downloadable software products after download
  • Gift cards and promotional credits
  • Domain names and SSL certificates
  • Third-party marketplace purchases
  • Services violating our Terms of Service
  • Accounts terminated for violation of policies

6. Partial Refunds

Partial refunds may be issued in the following circumstances:

  • Service disruptions exceeding our SLA guarantees
  • Billing errors or overcharges
  • Partially delivered services
  • Pro-rated refunds for annual plans

7. Dispute Resolution

7.1 Internal Review

If your refund request is denied, you can request a review by:

  • Providing additional information supporting your claim
  • Requesting escalation to a senior manager
  • Submitting evidence of service issues

7.2 Chargeback Policy

Important: Filing a chargeback instead of requesting a refund may result in:

  • Immediate account suspension
  • Loss of access to all services
  • Inability to make future purchases
  • Collection proceedings for disputed amounts

8. Special Circumstances

8.1 Free Trials

  • Free trials convert to paid subscriptions automatically
  • Cancel before trial ends to avoid charges
  • No refund needed for free trial periods

8.2 Promotional Offers

  • Discounted services may have different refund terms
  • Bundle packages are refunded as a whole
  • Promotional credits are non-refundable

8.3 Enterprise Contracts

  • Subject to individually negotiated terms
  • May require written notice period
  • Penalties may apply for early termination

9. Data Retention After Cancellation

  • Account data retained for 30 days after cancellation
  • You can request immediate deletion
  • Some data retained for legal compliance
  • Backups may persist for up to 90 days

10. Re-activation

After cancellation:

  • Accounts can be reactivated within 30 days
  • Previous settings and data may be restored
  • New pricing may apply
  • Promotional offers may not be available
